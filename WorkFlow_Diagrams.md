        (System shows timestamp: 2:16 PM)

        Debtor has opened and read the message
        (Can be immediate or days later)
        (System shows exact timestamp: 2:47 PM)

        FAILED ✗
        Message could not be delivered
        (Invalid number, phone off, WhatsApp not installed)
        System notifies staff immediately
        Can try alternate contact method

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Factor:                  Email              WhatsApp
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Read rate:              20%                 80%
Time to response:       2-4 hours           10-30 minutes
Professional feel:      Formal              Personal
Engagement:             Low                 High
Proof of reading:       Limited             Exact timestamp
Preference (debtors):   Low                 High

IMPACT: WhatsApp messages get 4x higher read rates = faster responses
        = faster payment arrangements = better cash flow
