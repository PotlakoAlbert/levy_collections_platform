## THREE FEATURES (In Plain English)

### 1. **Promise to Pay** (Payment Agreements)
*What it does:* Automatically creates and tracks payment arrangements with debtors
*Benefit:* Saves 35 minutes per case, ensures legal documentation
*Legal value:* Proof of agreed terms if disputes arise

### 2. **Generate Tasks** (Automated Case Workflow)
*What it does:* Creates checklist of required steps at each legal stage
*Benefit:* No steps missed, cases move faster, staff doesn't forget
*Legal value:* Ensures compliance with NCR Act procedures

### 3. **WhatsApp Messaging** (Client Communication)
*What it does:* Send messages to debtors from case management system, track everything
*Benefit:* 80% read rate (vs. 20% for email), faster responses
*Legal value:* Complete proof of all communications
